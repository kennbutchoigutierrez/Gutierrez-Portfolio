Project detail

Morning Briefing

A small automation that replaces a manual daily check-in. Every morning it queries the previous day's sign-ups from Supabase, sends them to an AI model to write a plain-language summary, and emails the digest to the team through Resend — all on a cron schedule, with no dashboard to open. It's a backend tool, so there's no public page; the screenshot in Work shows a sample briefing email.
